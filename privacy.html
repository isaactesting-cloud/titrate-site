Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

Titrate ("the App") is developed by Isaac Abraham. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

Titrate does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you enter — including child profiles, medication logs, daily observations, and growth measurements — is stored locally on your device only.

No Accounts or Registration

The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use it.

No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not use analytics services, advertising frameworks, or any third-party tracking tools. No usage data is collected or shared.

No Network Communication

The App does not communicate with external servers. It functions entirely offline. The only network activity that may occur is through standard iOS platform services (such as checking for app updates through the App Store), which are governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Local Notifications

The App may send local notifications to remind you to log daily observations. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device and do not involve any external service.

Data Export

You may choose to export your data as a JSON file or generate PDF reports. These files are created locally on your device. How you share or store these files after export is under your control and not governed by this policy.

Children's Privacy

While the App is designed to help parents track medication for their children, it does not collect or transmit any data about children or anyone else. All information remains on your device.

Data Deletion

You can delete any or all data within the App at any time. Uninstalling the App will permanently remove all stored data from your device.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at isaacabrahamtesting@gmail.com.
