Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers or the cloud.

Can I transfer my data to a new phone?

You can export your data as a JSON file from the Settings tab and import it on your new device.

How do I generate a report for my doctor?

Go to the Report tab, select a date range, and tap Generate. You can share the PDF directly from the app.

Can I track multiple children?

Yes. You can add and manage separate profiles for each child from the Settings tab.

Is Titrate a medical device?

No. Titrate is an organizational tool to help you track observations and communicate with your child's healthcare provider. It does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations.
